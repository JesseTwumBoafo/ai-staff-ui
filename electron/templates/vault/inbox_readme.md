# Inbox

Drop new files here for triage. This is the front door to your operating system:
anything you are not sure where to file goes here first, and gets routed to the
right pillar later.

The numbered pillars alongside this folder are:

- 0. Inbox: untriaged items (this folder).
- 1. Projects: active, time-bound work.
- 2. Areas: ongoing responsibilities.
- 3. Resources: reference material.
- 4. Archive: finished or inactive items.
- 5. Notes: your own notes and thinking.
- 6. Reviews: periodic reviews.

Your AI staff live under `8. Agents`. The gap from 6 to 8 is deliberate.
