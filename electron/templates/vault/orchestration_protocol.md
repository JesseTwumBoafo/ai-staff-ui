# Orchestration protocol

A starting stub for how work flows through your AI staff. Adjust it to suit how
you like to work.

1. You give a brief in plain language.
2. Your manager decides which specialist should handle it.
3. The specialist does the work, reading only from folders you have granted.
4. A reviewer checks the draft before it reaches you.
5. Nothing is saved or sent outside a review gate you approve.

This stub was deployed with your operating system. Expand it over time.
