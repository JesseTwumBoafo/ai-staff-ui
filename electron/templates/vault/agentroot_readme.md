# Agent root

This folder is the home of your AI operating system. It was deployed by the
Your AI Staff app (version {{APP_VERSION}}) on {{DATE}} for {{OWNER_NAME}}.

What lives here:

- `team/team_index.md`: the roster of your AI staff, seeded from the app.
- `bkm/sessions/`: a log file per completed run, written by the app.
- `bkm/sops/`: standard operating procedures (stubs to start; fill them in over time).
- `bkm/tasks/open.md`: the open-tasks ledger.
- `6. Outputs/`: where deliverables land, in a drafts-to-reviewed pipeline.

The app is the live system. These files are readable mirrors and logs that the
app reads and writes through folder access you have granted. Nothing here is
edited without an action you take in the app.
